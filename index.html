    How It Works

    🔍

    1. Paste

    Drop any URL into the input. LinkDetoxer instantly parses every parameter, fragment, and hidden tracker embedded in the link.

    🎯

    2. Detect

    Our database of 2500+ tracking signatures is matched against your URL, catching trackers from Google, Meta, TikTok, and hundreds more.

    🧹

    3. Clean

    Tracking junk is surgically removed while keeping every parameter the destination site actually needs to function properly.

    🔒

    4. Private

    100% client-side. Zero data leaves your browser. No logs, no analytics, no external requests. Your links stay yours.
